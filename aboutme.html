About Me

Mindy Romero is a full-time student at The College of New Jersey pursuing a career in Interactive Multimedia along with Photography and Fine Arts.


Born in Trenton, New Jersey she attended Trenton Central High School and earned a diploma in Applied Engineering in 2008. She has always had a passion for the arts and gained in interest in computing. Mindy is expected to graduate in May 2015 and hopes to reach a career that will serve her community.


Throughout her college career she learned various mediums within her Interactive Multimedia study including basic coding, web design, light film editing in Final Cut Pro, electronic music with Logic Pro 9, Adobe Photoshop & Illustrator, and Digital Photography.
